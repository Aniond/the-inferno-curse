Guilds + Church System — Managing Limbo Corruption

1200-1300 Florence • All major guilds + the Church as ways to handle absorbed abilities and Limbo infection

Core Concept

You absorb abilities from monsters (souls). This infects you (and Florence) with Limbo. The more equipped/higher the abilities, the stronger the manifestations (water, shadows, random battles).

The major guilds and the Church give you ways to **reinterpret or purify** those abilities instead of just living with the corruption.

How the Systems Interact with Abilities

Church Purification (Holy Variant)
Before: "Shadow Claw" — +5 damage, +2 STR while equipped, +1% corruption per use

After (Purified): "Sanctified Claw" — +4 damage, +1 FTH while equipped, no corruption, small self-heal on kill
Identity changes. Power is re-framed as holy. Lower raw strength, gains defensive or restorative qualities.
Guild Reinterpretation (Trade/Martial Variant)
Before: "Shadow Claw" (infernal theme, high corruption)

After (Arte dei Fabbri): "Forged Talon Gauntlet" — +5 damage, no corruption on use, requires specific weapon equipped, can be upgraded with materials
Stripped of supernatural elements. Becomes a grounded martial/crafting technique. Can tie into guild reputation and crafting systems.

Player Progression Loop

  1. Absorb ability → gains power + hidden Limbo infection
  2. Equip it → manifestations grow (water darkens, shadows move, random battles increase)
  3. Take it to a Guild or the Church
  4. Pay cost (reputation, money, quest, materials, or temporary loss of power)
  5. Get a reinterpreted or purified version → corruption reduced or removed, new theme/tags, possibly new mechanics
  6. World effects lessen as your personal Limbo load decreases

Strengths of this system

  • Gives meaning to every absorbed ability (you can’t just stack them forever)
  • Deeply ties player power to the historical guilds and the Church
  • Creates natural side content (guild quests, reputation, crafting chains)
  • Purification feels meaningful — the ability changes identity
  • Direct mechanical way to fight back against Limbo infection
