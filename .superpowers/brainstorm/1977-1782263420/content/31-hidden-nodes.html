Florence Map – Hidden Nodes Unlocked by Quests

Core nodes + hidden nodes that appear after completing specific quests (often Church or Guild related).

Base Florence Nodes (Always Visible)
  • Ponte Vecchio – Bridge district, trade, water effects.
  • The Old Market – Merchants, Guild hub, alleys.
  • Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore – Church center, purification, Focus ability.
  • The Docks – Port, boats, smuggling, strong water corruption.
Hidden Nodes (Quest-Unlocked)

These start invisible or greyed-out on the overview map. Completing certain quests (usually tied to Church reputation or specific Guild lines) makes them appear and become accessible.

Example Hidden Nodes

  • The Forgotten Crypts – Underground burial area. Unlocked via Church quest investigating Limbo-tainted dead. High corruption, unique holy or corrupted orbs.
  • The Smuggler's Warrens – Hidden alleys beneath the Old Market. Unlocked via Guild quest. Good for black-market reinterpretations, riskier but higher rewards.
  • The Abandoned Bell Tower – Overlooks the Cathedral. Unlocked after a major purification quest. Offers strong vantage for focus abilities and Limbo Square detection.

How It Works on the Overview

• Overview shows the 4 core nodes connected by roads.
• Hidden nodes appear as faded or "locked" icons until the quest condition is met.
• Once unlocked, they connect via new roads or secret paths and can be entered like normal nodes.
• Entering loads the usual small 3D map (free explore, grid combat on engagement, Limbo Squares inside).
• Some hidden nodes may have higher base corruption or unique Limbo Square behaviors (e.g. chained teleports or enemy ambushes on arrival).

Integration with Systems

Church Reputation
Many hidden nodes are gated behind Church quests/reputation. Higher reputation can also make some nodes appear earlier or with better starting conditions (lower corruption on entry).

World Corruption
Hidden nodes often start with higher corruption once unlocked (they were "sealed" by Limbo). They can spread corruption to connected visible nodes if ignored.

Saint vs Sinner
• Saint players unlock more "holy" hidden nodes (crypts, towers) that offer purification bonuses and lower personal Sanity cost.
• Sinner players unlock darker hidden nodes (warrens, ruins) that give raw power orbs but increase world corruption faster.

Ability Tree / Orbs
Hidden nodes are excellent for unique or rare orbs that aren't available in the main 4 nodes. Tree nodes can be tied to specific hidden locations (e.g. "orbs found in the Crypts gain +X holy damage").

Limbo Squares
Hidden nodes can have denser or more deceptive squares (still invisible unless you have Focus). The "think you're safe" teleport can be especially punishing here.

Strengths

  • Rewards questing and Church/Guild engagement with new explorable space.
  • Makes the city feel like it grows and changes based on your actions.
  • Great for hiding powerful or story-relevant orbs behind progression.
  • Fits the "Limbo is spreading" theme – some areas were sealed and only open when you dig deeper.

Things to watch

  • Make sure players don't miss too much if they ignore Church quests.
  • Balance so hidden nodes aren't strictly required for power, but offer meaningful variety.
