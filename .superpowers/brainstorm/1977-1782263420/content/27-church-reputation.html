Church Reputation System — Quests, Perks, and Progression

You do quests for the Church, gain reputation, and unlock extra perks. This is the main way to lean into the Saint path and manage Limbo corruption.

Core Loop

1. Accept Church quests (investigations, purifying corrupted zones, protecting holy sites, dealing with Limbo-tainted monsters, etc.).

2. Complete them to gain Church Reputation.

3. Reputation tiers unlock permanent or semi-permanent perks.

4. Higher reputation also improves your standing with the Church, affecting dialogue, prices, and access to special services.

Example Reputation Tiers & Perks

Tier 1 (Neutral / Low Rep)
• Basic access to Church services (purification available but expensive in reputation or coin).
• Learn basic Focus ability (see Limbo Squares).

Tier 2 (Respected)
• Purification costs reduced (less weakening of the ability, or lower reputation hit).
• Improved Focus (wider range, passive mode, or lower sanity cost).
• Small holy bonuses to equipped orbs (e.g. +damage vs Limbo creatures).

Tier 3 (Devout)
• Can request "blessed" reinterpretations of abilities (hybrid holy + original flavor with reduced corruption).
• Focus can suppress nearby Limbo Squares temporarily.
• Church provides occasional holy orbs or materials as rewards.
• World corruption in Florence decreases slightly faster when you are in the area.

Tier 4 (Saintly)
• Major perks: Abilities purified here gain unique holy effects without as much power loss.
• Permanent reduction to your personal Sanity cost on holy orbs.
• Ability to "consecrate" tree nodes (making them more effective for Saint path).
• Strong influence on city events (can help push back Limbo manifestations in certain districts).

Integration with Existing Systems

Single Target Template
Holy versions of Single Target abilities (Damage or Heal) benefit directly from Church reputation. Higher tiers can improve base_heal, stat scaling, or add anti-Limbo effects while keeping the corruption reduction.

Orb Tree
Church reputation can unlock or improve Saint-aligned tree nodes. Stacking holy orbs becomes more attractive because perks offset the power loss from purification.

Saint vs Sinner
Church reputation is the main mechanical support for the Saint path. Going deep into Church quests makes it easier to stay "clean" (lower personal Sanity cost, fewer world corruption effects) at the cost of potentially forgoing some raw power from Sinner-style stacking.

World Corruption
High Church reputation can passively slow the spread of Limbo effects in Florence (fewer Limbo Squares, slower water darkening, etc.). This is one of the visible rewards for choosing the Saint path.

Equippable Orbs
You still equip orbs normally. Church perks make holy versions more competitive in your loadout.

Why this fits

  • Gives the Church real long-term progression instead of a one-time service.
  • Reinforces the Saint vs Sinner choice with tangible gameplay differences.
  • Creates side content (Church quests) that directly impacts the main corruption systems.
  • Makes reputation feel meaningful beyond just "numbers go up."
  • Works well with the hidden corruption design — you see the world improve rather than staring at a bar.
