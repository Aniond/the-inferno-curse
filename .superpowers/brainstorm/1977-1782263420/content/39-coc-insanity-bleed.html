Call of Cthulhu-Style Insanity Bleed

World Corruption doesn't just affect Florence — it bleeds into the character's mind. You start to see the world tearing apart, and it affects you personally.

Your Idea

"We could go COC insanity. Seeing the world torn apart by Corruption bleeds into the Charecter"

This makes Sanity the personal, visible (to the player) mental toll caused by exposure to a corrupted world.

How the Bleed Works

World Corruption Events → Personal Sanity Hits

• Stepping on a Limbo Square (teleport into enemies) → Sanity loss
• Witnessing or causing a random battle due to corruption → Sanity loss
• Hearing NPC rumors about murders/missing people caused by your neglect → Sanity loss
• High water corruption or moving shadows in a node → Sanity loss over time while present
• Using a high-Corruption orb in a high-corruption area → amplified Sanity hit

Personal Effects (player feels these)
• Low Sanity: Subtle unease, occasional whispers, slightly off combat feedback.
• Medium Sanity: Hallucinations during exploration, orbs sometimes feel "wrong", increased risk of bad teleports.
• High Sanity: Intrusive thoughts, temporary loss of control, orbs can misfire or draw extra corruption.
• Extreme: Character starts acting against player's interests in small ways (dialogue options change, forced risky actions).

Integration with Flat Skill Values

Each orb still has two flat values (as you confirmed):

  • base_sanity_cost — direct personal cost when used or equipped.
  • base_corruption_value — how much it contributes to the world's Corruption.

The bleed happens on top of that: high world Corruption (driven by orbs + player decisions) causes ongoing Sanity loss through exposure. Using orbs in a corrupted world accelerates the bleed.

Interaction with Saint vs Sinner

Sinner Path
Embraces the bleed. Tree nodes that increase power on stacked orbs can also increase how much world Corruption "feeds back" as personal Sanity loss — more power, more madness, more power from the madness.

Saint Path
Actively fights the bleed. Church purification reduces both the orb's base_sanity_cost and its contribution to world Corruption. Focus ability and holy orbs slow or reverse personal Sanity loss in corrupted areas.

Why this fits the game

  • Perfect Dante/Limbo feel: the world is falling apart, and so are you.
  • Makes every negative decision or stacked orb have a personal, accumulating price.
  • Creates genuine horror and roleplay without numbers on screen (hidden, shown through effects and NPC reactions).
  • Gives the Church real power: not just lowering numbers, but literally saving your mind.
  • Strong differentiation between paths: Sinner leans into the madness for power; Saint tries to stay sane while saving the city.

Still needs decisions

  • How fast does the bleed happen? (per exposure event, over time in corrupted nodes, on every ability use?)
  • Can the player ever reduce personal Sanity without Church intervention, or is the Church the only real cure?
  • Should extreme Sanity loss ever force mechanical changes (like temporary loss of control over an orb)?
