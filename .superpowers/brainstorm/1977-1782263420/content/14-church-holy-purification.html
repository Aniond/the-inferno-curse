Church Purification — Holy Variant Creation

Confirmed: The Church transforms a corrupted ability into a holy version that changes its identity.

Core Mechanic

When you bring a corrupted ability to the Church, they perform a purification ritual. This does not just strip corruption — it creates a new "holy" version of the ability with a changed name, theme, and often altered effects.

Example Transformation (Single Target)

Corrupted Version (Limbo-infected)
Shadow Bolt
Type: Single Target Damage
Base Damage: 8 + STR scaling
Theme: Infernal / Shadow
Equip Effect: +1 Corruption weight
On Use: Deals damage + small Limbo manifestation increase (shadows lengthen locally)
Carries the weight of the absorbed soul.
Holy Version (after Church purification)
Holy Strike (or "Sanctified Bolt", "Radiant Thrust", etc.)
Type: Single Target Damage
Base Damage: 7 + FTH or STR scaling (tradeoff for purity)
Theme: Sacred / Holy
Equip Effect: 0 Corruption weight (or small reduction to total Limbo)
On Use: Deals damage + small self-blessing or bonus vs undead/shades
The soul is redeemed and re-framed. Power feels different.

Design Rules for Holy Variants

  • Name Change: Always renames the ability to reflect its new holy identity (Shadow Bolt → Holy Strike).
  • Theme Shift: Changes from infernal/corrupt tags to sacred/holy tags. This can affect future interactions (Church reputation, certain guild reinterpretations, enemy reactions).
  • Corruption Reduction: Removes or heavily reduces the ability’s contribution to your hidden Limbo infection. World manifestations (water color, shadows, random battles) become less severe.
  • Mechanical Tradeoff: Not a strict upgrade. Holy versions often trade raw damage or speed for utility, healing, or anti-Limbo effects.
  • Permanent Identity: Once purified, the ability stays in its holy form (unless further altered by a guild).

Why this fits the setting

  • Matches Dante’s Florence + Limbo theme — the Church is actively pushing back against Hell’s influence.
  • Gives real narrative weight to "purification" instead of just "remove debuff".
  • Creates choice: keep the raw corrupted power or take the redeemed but different holy version.
  • Holy versions can have unique interactions with Limbo manifestations (e.g. holy damage slows or banishes shades).
