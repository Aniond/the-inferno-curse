Orb Skill Tree – Sanity (Player) vs Corruption (World)

Sanity = your personal corruption toll. Corruption = the world/NPC side (Limbo infection in Florence, NPC behavior, environmental effects).

Your Clarification

"sanity = corruption. Sanity is player based. COrruption is wolrd based. npc etc"

Stacking duplicates of the same orb in the tree primarily increases the sanity cost (personal price you pay), while the world corruption is a separate but related system.

How Stacking Works Now

Shadow Claw (Single Target Damage)

Base skill (1st copy in tree):
• Damage: base + STR scaling
• Sanity cost: base value (player pays this to use or sustain)
• Corruption contribution: base (feeds world effects)

2nd copy slotted:
• Damage: + increment
• Sanity cost: base + 1 (or more)
• Corruption contribution: still base (or slight increase)

3rd copy slotted:
• Damage: higher
• Sanity cost: base + 2
• Corruption: still mostly base

You get more power on that orb, but it becomes personally more expensive to use (sanity).

Player Sanity vs World Corruption

Sanity (Player-based)

  • Personal cost to the character.
  • Ramps with duplicate stacks in the tree.
  • Could manifest as extra resource cost, drain over time, temporary debuffs, forced hesitation, or mental strain when using the orb.
  • Directly tied to the Saint vs Sinner choice (Saints will hate high sanity cost; Sinners will tolerate or lean into it).

Corruption (World-based)

  • Affects Florence, NPCs, environment (water color, shadows, random battles, NPC behavior, Limbo bleed).
  • Driven more by total number/strength of equipped orbs + actions, not purely by how many duplicates of one orb you stack.
  • Church purification and Guild reinterpretation reduce this world corruption.

Integration with Equip + Tree

An orb can still be equipped for active use while multiple copies are invested in the tree.

The tree stack increases the sanity cost of using that ability concept.

Tree nodes can interact with sanity cost (e.g. some nodes reduce sanity cost for orbs in that cluster at the cost of something else; dark nodes might increase power but also sanity cost).

Why this split is powerful

  • Keeps corruption as the thematic world horror (Limbo infecting Florence).
  • Gives stacking a clear personal downside (sanity) that the player feels directly.
  • Reinforces Saint vs Sinner: Saints will work to keep sanity low (via purification, careful stacking); Sinners will push sanity high for power.
  • Allows different levers: you can reduce world corruption without fully removing the personal sanity cost, or vice versa.
