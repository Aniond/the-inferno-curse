Church Focus Ability — Progression-Gated Perception

At higher levels you visit the Church. The priest teaches you an ability to see through the corruption (focus).

Your Rule

"You go to the church at higher levels. he teaches you an ablity to see through the corruption"

This makes the detection of Limbo Squares a deliberate late-game unlock tied to the Church system.

How It Works

Early Game (No Focus)
• Limbo Squares are completely invisible.
• You only discover them by stepping on one (random teleport into danger).
• Pure paranoia and world corruption punishment.

Higher Levels — Church Visit
• You go to the Church (unlocked or story-gated at higher levels).
• The priest teaches you the "Focus" ability.
• Once learned, you can reveal Limbo Squares when focusing.
• Squares now glow or distort visibly within focus range.

Focus Behavior
• Can be passive while the ability/orb is equipped, or active toggle.
• May have a small Sanity or MP cost to maintain.
• Upgradable later via the ability tree or further Church visits.

Integration with Existing Systems

World Corruption Scaling
Even with focus, higher world corruption = more squares and more dangerous teleports. The ability lets you see them, but doesn't remove the hazard.

Saint vs Sinner
• Strongly Saint-aligned unlock (Church teaches it).
• Sinner players can still learn it, but it may feel "off" or come with a small corruption penalty until purified.
• Once learned, Saint-path tree nodes can make focus cheaper, wider range, or even suppress squares temporarily.
• Sinner-path nodes might turn focus into an aggressive tool (reveal squares to force enemies onto them).

Ability Tree & Orbs
• The Church-taught Focus can be treated as an orb itself or a node unlock.
• Early tree nodes: basic focus range.
• Mid nodes: passive focus while moving, or mark squares.
• Late nodes: share focus with nearby allies, or use focus to gain combat buffs after detecting a square.

Single Target Template Tie-in
A Single Target "Clarity" or "Revelation" orb could grant or enhance this focus as a secondary effect (e.g. "while focused, reveal nearby Limbo Squares and gain +10% accuracy").

Church/Guild Synergy
• First visit teaches the basic ability.
• Later visits (or Guild reinterpretation) can upgrade the focus or combine it with other orbs.
• Purification of other abilities at the Church can also reduce the sanity cost of maintaining focus.

Why this is strong

  • Makes the Church feel like a real progression and narrative hub.
  • Delays powerful perception until the player has invested in the world systems.
  • Perfectly reinforces the Saint vs Sinner theme (Church = Light path tool).
  • Gives exploration a clear power curve tied to corruption management.
  • Creates a natural "aha" moment when you first learn to see the invisible danger.

Still to decide

  • Can Sinner players get an alternative "dark focus" version from somewhere else?
