Each Skill/Orb Has Flat Sanity + Corruption Values

Confirmed: Every ability has two flat base values — one for player Sanity, one for world Corruption. These are properties of the skill itself.

Your Rule

"Have sanity skill + corruption makes sense. Its a flat value of the skill."

Each orb you absorb carries its own fixed Sanity cost (player) and Corruption contribution (world). These do not start at zero — they are baked into the orb at the moment of absorption.

Example Flat Values on Orbs

Shadow Claw (Single Target Damage)
base_damage = 8
scaling_stat = "STR"
base_sanity_cost = 3
base_corruption_value = 2

base_damage = 6

base_corruption_value = 0

Inferno Bolt (Area Effect)
base_damage = 12 (in 3x3)
base_sanity_cost = 5
base_corruption_value = 4

How These Values Are Used

When Equipped
The orb adds its base_sanity_cost to your current personal Sanity load.
It also contributes its base_corruption_value to the world's current Corruption level (affects Limbo Squares density, shadow behavior, NPC tension, etc.).

When Used in Combat
You pay the current Sanity cost of the orb (can be modified by tree nodes or duplicates).
The orb's Corruption value is applied to the world at the moment of use (or slightly after).

Tree Stacking (Duplicates)
When you slot multiple copies of the same orb type in the tree:
• Damage/power increases.
• The effective Sanity cost of that orb line increments.
• The base_corruption_value may stay flat or get a small multiplier depending on how aggressive the node is.

Church Purification
Creates a new holy version with:
• Lower base_sanity_cost
• Significantly reduced or zero base_corruption_value
• Usually lower raw power (as previously established)

Integration with Saint vs Sinner

Sinner Path
• You embrace orbs with high base_sanity_cost and high base_corruption_value.
• Tree nodes that increase power on stacked orbs will also increase the Sanity cost further.
• You are consciously choosing to carry more personal Sanity load and accelerate world Corruption.

Saint Path
• You prioritize orbs with low Sanity and low Corruption (or get them purified at the Church).
• Church reputation perks can further reduce Sanity costs on holy orbs.
• You actively try to keep both your personal Sanity and the city's Corruption low.

Why flat values per skill work well

  • Every absorbed orb has an inherent "weight" — some souls are heavier than others.
  • Makes the moment of absorption meaningful ("this one will cost me Sanity and spread more Corruption").
  • Clear lever for Church purification (they reduce the values).
  • Stacking in the tree becomes a deliberate escalation of both personal and world cost.
  • Easy to balance per ability type (Single Target vs Area vs Buff vs Trait can have different typical ranges).

Still needs tuning

  • How much does a duplicate stack increase the Sanity cost? (flat +1, percentage, etc.)
  • Does using the orb apply its full base_corruption_value every time, or only the first time per encounter?
