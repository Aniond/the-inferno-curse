Skills Grant Sanity Cost — World Horror Amplifies It

Mixture confirmed: Each skill/orb has a flat base Sanity cost (player). Using it or having it equipped grants/increases Sanity. Seeing world Corruption (horror) amplifies the Sanity impact.

Your Rule

"it would be a mixure of both. Your skills grant sanity and seeing the world horor amplifies that"

Skills are the source of personal Sanity cost. The corrupted world makes that cost worse — more intense, more frequent, or with extra personal effects.

Flat Values per Skill (as locked earlier)

Every orb has two flat values:
base_sanity_cost = X (player personal toll)
base_corruption_value = Y (world contribution)

Example — Shadow Claw (Single Target Damage):
base_sanity_cost = 3
base_corruption_value = 2
base_damage = 8
scaling_stat = "STR"

How the Mixture Works

Skills Grant Sanity

  • Equipping an orb adds its base_sanity_cost to your current personal Sanity load.
  • Using the orb in combat pays the current Sanity cost (can be modified by tree stacks or nodes).
  • Stacking duplicates of the same orb in the tree increases both damage and the effective Sanity cost of that orb line.

World Horror Amplifies It

  • When world Corruption is high (Limbo Squares visible, shadows moving, water dark, random battles common), the Sanity cost of using skills becomes amplified.
  • Amplification examples (to be balanced later):
    • Higher effective cost (e.g. 3 becomes 5 in high corruption).
    • Extra personal effects (intrusive thoughts, temporary loss of control, orbs feel "wrong").
    • Increased bleed into world (using the skill while horrified makes nearby Corruption effects stronger).
  • Low world Corruption = skills cost closer to their base Sanity value. The world isn't fighting you as hard.

Tree Stacking & Amplification

Placing multiple copies of the same orb in the tree:

  • Increases damage/power of that orb concept.
  • Increments the Sanity cost of using it.
  • When the world is horrified (high Corruption), the Sanity cost is amplified on top of the stacking increase.

Example: A 3x stacked Shadow Claw might cost 3 base + 2 from stacks = 5 Sanity normally. In a high-corruption node it might cost 7 or 8, plus risk of personal side effects.

Why the mixture works

  • Skills are the source of personal Sanity (player agency and cost).
  • World horror (Corruption) acts as an environmental amplifier — the same skill feels worse in a more broken world.
  • Creates natural tension: using power in a corrupted area hurts you more.
  • Fits Saint vs Sinner: Saints try to keep both low; Sinners stack power knowing the world will amplify their Sanity cost.
  • Church Focus ability and purification become powerful tools to reduce base Sanity and resist amplification.

Still to tune (as you noted, balance testing later)

  • Whether amplification only happens in high-corruption nodes or scales continuously.
  • How personal side effects from high Sanity + amplification manifest (visuals, forced actions, orb misfires).
