Single Target — Two Variants: Damage & Heal

Equipable model with separate but parallel templates for Attack and Heal

New Direction

You need two clear variants under the Single Target type. They share a lot of structure (because they are equipable abilities), but have important differences in effect, scaling, and feel.

Shared vs Variant-Specific Fields

Shared (same for both variants)

  • id, display_name, description
  • type = "single_target"
  • range, is_ranged, requires_line_of_sight
  • action_cost, mp_cost
  • corruption_on_equip, corruption_on_use
  • theme_tags, rarity
  • equip logic (must be equipped to use)

Damage-specific

  • base_damage + scaling_stat (STR/INT/etc)
  • damage_type
  • ct_target_delay (usually positive to slow enemy)
  • apply_status (e.g. bleed, poison)

Heal-specific

  • base_heal + scaling_stat (FTH usually)
  • heal_type
  • ct_target_bonus (helps ally get their turn faster)
  • can_target_self = true

Advantages of two explicit variants

  • Clean data — no "if category == attack then..." everywhere
  • Easy to balance separately (heals often safer, lower corruption)
  • Monsters can offer one or the other (brigand gives damage, cultist might give heal)
  • Future-proofs for area versions later

Still need to decide

  • Do damage and heal share the same equip slots, or are they in different pools?
  • Should heals ever have corruption_on_equip?
  • Can a single ability be both? (probably not for now)
