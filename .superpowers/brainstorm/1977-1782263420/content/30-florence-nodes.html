Florence Nodes – Persona-Style Overview

4–5 nodes for Florence. Overview map with roads. Entering a node loads a small 3D explorable area.

Confirmed Nodes for Florence
  • Ponte Vecchio – The iconic bridge district. Shopping, views of the Arno, narrow streets. Good early-game access.
  • The Old Market – Bustling trade area. Merchants, crowds, potential for hidden alleys and shady deals. Guild presence.
  • Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore – The Duomo area. Religious center, Church influence strong. Purification and Focus ability access.
  • The Docks – Riverside/port area. Trade, smuggling, boats. Water corruption effects more prominent here.

(Room for a 5th node depending on the city – e.g. a residential quarter, government/palace area, or a more dangerous slum/tannery district.)

Overview Map Flow

• Central "Florence" overview screen.
• Nodes connected by roads/paths (visual lines the player travels along).
• Player selects or walks to a node on the overview.
• Loading into the 3D map for that node (small-to-medium 3D space like the current test map).
• Free movement inside the node. Limbo Squares (invisible until focused) can force random teleports and combat.
• Exit back to overview when done.

Corruption & Systems Integration

World Corruption per Node
Each node can have its own corruption "heat" that contributes to the global city level. Corruption spreads along roads if left unchecked.

Unique Flavor per Node (examples)
• Ponte Vecchio: Water corruption very visible (Arno darkens). Limbo Squares often teleport across the bridge or into the river area.
• Old Market: Crowds + shadows. Random battles feel like ambushes in alleys. Good for Guild reinterpretation quests.
• Cathedral: Strong Church presence. Purification services here. Limbo Squares feel more "wrong" near holy ground.
• Docks: Smuggling, boats, water-heavy hazards. Possible fast travel by boat (risky at high corruption).

Exploration & Combat
Inside each 3D node: free movement until Limbo Square or enemy triggers tactical grid combat. Corruption increases square density and enemy aggression inside that node.

Strengths

  • Matches Persona navigation feel (clean overview + focused 3D areas).
  • Easy to layer unique corruption effects and side content per node.
  • Scales well: start with 4 nodes, add more as the story/city expands.
  • Clear hubs for Church (Cathedral) and Guilds (Old Market).
